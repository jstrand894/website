Elk Park and Badger Gulch - May 2025

Rhinusa Grids
Montana
Back to Elk Park to finish up building the exclosure fence, and visited Badger Gulch to set up trail cameras and retrieve weather data.
Published

May 21, 2025

Weather

⛅ Low 50s, partly sunny. Cool and pleasant with occasional sunbreaks.

Eric, Aden, and I drove back out to Elk Park to finish up building the exclosure fence. We then drove over to Badger Gulch for our first visit of 2025 to retrieve weather data and install the trail cameras.

Elk Park

We had leftover fencing from our South Dakota trip (exactly the right amount!) so we used that to wrap around the entire Elk Park exclosure. I also retreived images from the trail cameras I set up during our last visit on May 8, 2025.

Site activities

  • Finish building exclosure
  • Add stakes to control grid where slash piles make road hares unusable
  • Retrieve preliminary images from trail cameras

Badger Gulch

We previously set up the exclosure at Badger Gulch in November of 2024. We returned to the site to retrieve data logger data as well as set up the trail cameras.

Site activities

  • Install trail cameras
  • Retrieve weather data from data loggers

Trail camera images

Captured a couple cool things with our cameras after just two weeks!
